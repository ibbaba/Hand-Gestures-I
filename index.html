Drawing Canvas with Live Camera Feed

Status: Click "Start Camera" to begin

How to Use:

  • Point with your index finger - The app tracks your index fingertip
  • Make a fist - Stop drawing (pen up)
  • Point again - Start drawing (pen down)
  • Move your hand - Draw lines by moving your pointing finger
  • Clear Canvas - Use the button to clear your drawing

Note: Make sure you're in good lighting and your hand is clearly visible to the camera.
